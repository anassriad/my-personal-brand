Projects

Psychology Chatbot

A judgment-free AI-powered mental health assistant designed to provide insights into psychological disorders. The chatbot uses natural language processing to offer personalized support, helping users understand and manage mental health challenges. It's my effort to create a safe space for individuals seeking mental health insights without stigma.

Technologies used: Node.js, Express, OpenAI, JavaScript, NLP

Daily Quotes Reminder App

An app designed to deliver daily motivational quotes to inspire users. The app encourages positivity and helps individuals stay focused on their goals by providing thoughtful reminders that improve mindset and productivity. Whether you need a morning boost or a midday reminder, this app has got you covered.

Technologies used: React, JavaScript, CSS

Marathon Morocco

A platform for organizing and tracking marathon events in Morocco, designed to enhance the marathon experience for both organizers and participants. The website allows users to register for events, track their progress, and interact with the marathon community.

Technologies used: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js
